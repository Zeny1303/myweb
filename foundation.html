Classroom Foundation Courses

Resolute Educational Institute proudly showcases a cadre of instructors hailing from prestigious institutions like the IITs and the NITs. This esteemed team is dedicated to delivering comprehensive guidance on both the JEE Main and Advanced syllabi, emphasizing profound understanding alongside adept problem-solving methodologies and strategic exam approaches. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, our faculty ensures students receive holistic preparation, equipping them with the skills and insights necessary to excel in these highly competitive examinations. At Resolute, we cultivate a nurturing environment where aspirations meet expertise, empowering each student to realize their full potential.

Our Courses for 9th and 10thGraders:


:)  Curriculum: Comprehensive coverage of fundamental concepts in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics aligned with JEE syllabus.


:)  Teaching Methodology: Interactive lectures, problem-solving sessions, and regular assessments to strengthen conceptual understanding.


:)  Study Materials: Customized study materials designed to build a strong foundation, including textbooks, workbooks, and practice papers.


:)  Additional Resources: One to one doubt-clearing sessions, and periodic parent-teacher meetings for progress monitoring.


:)   Facilities:Pick and drop facilities, daily attendance system, regular counselling sessions
